The Problem

Why does Arc exist?

Existing blockchains weren't designed with stablecoins in mind. Arc fixes that from the ground up.

⛓️

Wrong foundations

Existing blockchains weren't built for stablecoins — they were retrofitted. That means unnecessary complexity, friction, and risk baked into every transaction.

🎲

Gas fees in volatile tokens

On most chains, you need ETH, SOL, or MATIC just to pay fees. That's a problem when you're moving dollars — your gas cost itself becomes unpredictable.

⏳

Settlement takes too long

Real finance moves fast. Traditional blockchains can take minutes or more to finalize. That's unacceptable for institutions moving billions of dollars.

The Solution

What Arc actually does

Arc rethinks the blockchain from scratch — purpose-built for the way modern money actually works.

01
💵

Gas in USDC

Pay transaction fees in USDC — no volatile tokens needed. Predictable costs, always.
